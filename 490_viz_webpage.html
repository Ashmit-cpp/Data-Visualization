The deaths worldwide have begun to plateau as more resources like vaccines, highly effective PPE, and treaments have been discovered. Also, the access to all these resoruces has been an equally important challenge that needed to be overcome. Along with this, the coutries seeing the highest number of deaths have either lacked resources like India during the Delta variant and Peru during the lambda variant. Another factor that caused an increase of deaths was the lack of knowlege of how to prevent the virus from spreading like China when COVID-19 was first discovered. Finally, the political climate of the countries impacted played a large role as seen with the United States.

The United States especially has seen the highest number of overall deaths due to a few main factors. The first factor being the political climate where the people and government was deeply divided on the correct approach to prevent the virus from spreading. There was initially hesitency from the government to place a strict lockdown and mask madates and there was a strong push back from the people once this was implemented. Also now with the wide spread availability of the vaccine there is a sigificant portions of the popultion that are hestitent to recieve it.
